About Aroliex

Innovation through Blockchain Technology

Aroliex (ARO) is an innovation in the field of coffee industry development that integrates the collaboration of all stake holders to connect through the intermediary of blockchain technology.

Because there are many factors that hinder the Coffee Industry from collaborating with all stakeholders in its ecosystem, Aroliex (ARO) was born with a Vision & Mission to integrate all parties involved in this field through one unified ecosystem connected via Blockchain technology. Through smart contracts from Polygon Technology, in the future it is hoped that Aroliex (ARO) can become a solution for the development of the Coffee Industry that is integrated in one ecosystem that needs each other.
