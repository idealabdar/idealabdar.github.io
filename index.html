About Aroliex

Innovation through Blockchain Technology

Aroliex is a crypto token and digital asset that is connected to the local and global coffee ecosystem through the integration of knowledge, economics, technology, society and the environment.

We believe coffee is not just a magical seed, an economic commodity that connects between history and civilization across the countries, but it is a part of an ecosystem of life that is interconnected and bound to each other.
Therefore, it is necessary to brainstorm problems in the chain based on facts and real-time data, which is expected to give rise to innovation to the solution and sustainable for the project.
